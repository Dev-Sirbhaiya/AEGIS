# SOP: Drone / UAV Intrusion

## Severity: HIGH-CRITICAL (Level 4-5)

## Trigger Conditions
- Drone detected within 5km of airport boundary by radar or visual sighting
- Drone detected flying over runway, taxiway, or airside zone
- Drone reported by aircraft crew, ATC, or ground staff
- Anti-drone system alert triggered
- Member of public reports drone flying near airport
- Suspicious drone with camera or attached payload (higher risk)

## Immediate Actions
1. Notify ATC (Changi Approach) immediately — provide drone position, altitude, and direction of movement
2. Notify CAAS Duty Officer (Safety and Security division) — mandatory notification for airspace intrusion
3. Alert all aircraft on approach/departure via ATC — consider temporary ground stop if drone on runway path
4. Dispatch patrol officers to locate and identify drone operator on ground
5. Activate AEGIS CCTV tracking to follow drone movement — relay coordinates to officers
6. Attempt to identify drone registration number if visible via camera zoom
7. Do NOT attempt to physically intercept or shoot down the drone — APD authorization required

## Do NOT
- Do NOT attempt to disable, capture, or destroy the drone without APD/CAAS authorization
- Do NOT allow aircraft movements to continue if drone is on final approach path
- Do NOT assume drone is benign — treat all intrusions as potential threats until cleared
- Do NOT communicate threat details on unencrypted public channels

## Escalation Criteria
- Drone on runway or taxiway → Level 5, full ground stop, CAAS emergency notification
- Drone with attached object (potential payload) → Level 5, BDU + APD armed response
- Multiple drones simultaneously → Level 5, coordinated threat, ISD notification
- Drone operator located and uncooperative → Level 4, APD arrest, CAAS investigation
- Drone collides with aircraft or infrastructure → Level 5, AAIB notification, major incident protocol

## Post-Incident
- Submit mandatory Airspace Infringement Report to CAAS within 4 hours
- Preserve all CCTV footage and radar/sensor logs
- Coordinate with APD on drone operator identification and prosecution
- Review anti-drone system performance and detection thresholds
- Notify airlines affected by ground stop of final resolution time

# SOP: Unattended Baggage / Suspicious Item

## Severity: MEDIUM-HIGH (Level 3-4)

## Trigger Conditions
- Bag, package, or item left unattended for more than 15 minutes in public area
- Item reported as suspicious by passenger, staff, or officer
- Bag left near high-value targets: security screening, gate areas, VIP lounges
- Item with unusual features: wires, unusual smell, unexpected weight, sealed packaging
- Owner cannot be located after intercom announcement

## Immediate Actions
1. Establish visual cordon — do NOT touch the item; keep 30-meter distance
2. Make PA announcement: "Attention — the owner of a [item description] left at [location] please return to claim it immediately"
3. Check nearest CCTV to identify person who left the item and track their movements
4. Contact terminal floor staff and retail outlets near the item to check if owner is known
5. If owner not found within 10 minutes of discovery: notify Duty Manager and request APD attendance
6. If item has suspicious features: treat as Level 5 bomb threat → apply Bomb Threat SOP immediately

## Do NOT
- Do NOT touch, move, kick, or open the unattended item
- Do NOT allow members of public within 30-meter cordon
- Do NOT use mobile phones or radios within 15 meters if bomb threat suspected
- Do NOT dismiss reports from members of public without physical verification

## Escalation Criteria
- Item has suspicious features (wires, smell, unusual weight) → escalate to Level 5, Bomb Threat SOP
- Owner cannot be located after 20 minutes → escalate to Level 4, APD/BDU attendance required
- Item found in airside or restricted zone → escalate to Level 4, CAAS notification required
- Multiple unattended items simultaneously → escalate to Level 5, coordinated threat assessment

## Post-Incident
- Document item description, location, time found, time resolved
- If APD/BDU attended: obtain reference number and attach to AEGIS incident record
- Review CCTV footage and retain for 30 days minimum
- If item was abandoned intentionally: coordinate with ICA for passenger tracking
- Brief terminal team on reporting procedures

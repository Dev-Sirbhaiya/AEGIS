# SOP: Active Threat / Armed Individual

## Severity: CRITICAL (Level 5)

## Trigger Conditions
- Weapon (firearm, knife, bladed weapon) sighted on individual in terminal
- Reports of shots fired or stabbing in or near airport
- Active attack on passengers, staff, or officers in progress
- Intelligence alert for specific armed individual at airport
- Hostage situation reported

## Immediate Actions
1. Activate AMBER/RED threat level protocol immediately — notify all officers via radio (encrypted channel)
2. Notify APD, SPF, and Special Tasks Force (STF) immediately — ext 999 + direct SOC line
3. Initiate LOCKDOWN of affected zone — seal access points, lock airside doors
4. Direct all civilians to shelter-in-place or evacuate away from the threat (Run-Hide-Tell)
5. CCTV operator: track threat movement in real-time, relay position to officers every 30 seconds
6. DO NOT confront armed individual unless you are a trained tactical officer with authorization
7. Protect critical infrastructure: fuel stores, ATC, baggage screening, airport systems
8. Clear aircraft stands and taxiways — halt all aircraft movements in affected zone

## Do NOT
- Do NOT engage the armed individual unless trained and authorized (armed police only)
- Do NOT broadcast threat details on open/public radio channels
- Do NOT allow civilian evacuees back into the affected zone
- Do NOT allow media access to the incident area
- Do NOT negotiate unless a trained Crisis Negotiator is present

## Escalation Criteria
- Multiple armed individuals confirmed → Level 5 sustained, ISD/JPAM activation
- Casualties confirmed → Level 5, SCDF Mass Casualty + TTAMC activation
- Threat moves toward aircraft or ATC tower → Level 5, CAAS/RSAF immediate notification
- Explosives suspected in addition to weapons → Level 5, BDU + HAZMAT protocols activated

## Post-Incident
- Scene preserved until SPF Forensics clears
- Full incident report to MHA, CAAS, SPF within 2 hours
- Debrief with STF, APD, and SOC teams within 24 hours
- CCTV footage archived and handed to SPF investigation team
- Post-incident psychological support offered to all responding officers

# SOP: Lift / Escalator Breakdown with Trapped Passengers

## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM (Level 2)

## Trigger Conditions
- Lift alarm button activated
- Intercom call from passengers inside a stopped lift
- Lift doors fail to open at floor level
- Lift does not respond to button presses for more than 3 minutes
- Escalator emergency stop triggered or mechanical fault alarm
- Passenger reports feeling lift moving erratically or hearing unusual sounds

## Immediate Actions
1. Respond to lift location immediately — confirm number of persons trapped and their condition
2. Communicate with trapped persons via lift intercom: "Help is on the way, please stay calm"
3. Contact CAG Facilities Management / Lift Maintenance (ext 3000) for immediate technician dispatch
4. Do NOT attempt to open lift doors manually without technician authorization
5. If medical emergency inside lift: notify Airport Medical Services (ext 3911) simultaneously
6. Place physical barriers and signage at lift entrances on all floors to prevent additional entry
7. Activate alternative lift/escalator to relieve passenger flow in affected area
8. Monitor continuously via intercom until technician arrives

## Do NOT
- Do NOT force open lift doors (risk of persons falling into shaft)
- Do NOT advise passengers to attempt self-rescue by climbing out
- Do NOT abandon communication with trapped passengers at any point
- Do NOT allow other passengers to access the lift until fully cleared by technician

## Escalation Criteria
- Passenger has medical emergency inside lift → Level 3, trigger Medical Emergency SOP
- Lift falls or structural failure suspected → Level 3, evacuate floor area, SCDF notification
- Multiple lifts fail simultaneously (system-wide) → Level 3, activate contingency mobility plan
- Passenger becomes distressed, panicked, or threatens self-harm → Level 3, request psychological support

## Post-Incident
- Obtain technician incident report and attach to AEGIS record
- Notify passengers of resolution and issue apology/assistance if delayed significantly
- Log lift ID, fault type, response time, and resolution time
- Report recurring faults to CAG Facilities for preventive maintenance review
- Update out-of-service lift records in terminal operations system

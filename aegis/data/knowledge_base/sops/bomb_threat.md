# SOP: Bomb Threat / Suspicious Package

## Severity: CRITICAL (Level 5)

## Trigger Conditions
- Verbal bomb threat received via phone, intercom, or in person
- Written threat found on note, graffiti, or digital message
- Unattended bag or package left for more than 15 minutes in restricted area
- Package with suspicious features: wires, unusual odour, ticking, leaking substance
- Anonymous tip or intelligence about explosive device
- X-ray screening flags suspicious object in baggage

## Immediate Actions
1. Do NOT touch, move, or open the suspicious package
2. Establish minimum 100-meter cordon around the object immediately
3. Evacuate all persons from the zone — use emergency PA system if required
4. Notify APD/SPF Bomb Disposal Unit (BDU) immediately — ext 999 or direct line
5. Notify Terminal Duty Manager and CAAS Duty Officer
6. Suspend all flights departing from affected terminal gates
7. Preserve scene — do NOT allow re-entry until BDU clears the area
8. Activate Incident Command Post at pre-designated location

## Do NOT
- Do NOT touch, X-ray again, or attempt to open the suspicious package
- Do NOT use mobile phones or radios within 15 meters of the package (RF detonation risk)
- Do NOT assume it is a hoax — treat every threat as real until BDU confirms safe
- Do NOT evacuate to a secondary area without checking it is clear first
- Do NOT communicate threat details over open radio channels

## Escalation Criteria
- BDU confirms explosive device → Level 5 sustained, full terminal evacuation
- Multiple packages found → Level 5, notify ICA, CAAS, MOT
- Threat credibility confirmed by intelligence → Level 5, national emergency protocols
- Detonation occurs → Level 5, activate Mass Casualty and SCDF HAZMAT simultaneously

## Post-Incident
- Preserve all evidence — do NOT clean scene before police clearance
- Full written report to CAAS, APD, and MHA within 2 hours of all-clear
- Debrief all responding officers and supervisors within 24 hours
- Conduct security review of CCTV footage retention and access logs
- Review SOP with entire security team within 7 days
